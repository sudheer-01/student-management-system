  1. Configure Branches & Subjects: Define and manage branches, sections, and subjects for the academic years assigned to you.
  2. Approve Faculty Requests: Review subject allocation requests submitted by faculty and approve or reject them based on department needs.
  3. Manage Student Details: Add, update, and maintain student records including hall ticket numbers and section-wise allocation.
  4. Create & Manage Exams: Add exams, define maximum marks, and manage exam configurations for each section.
  5. Review Marks Update Requests: Verify and approve marks modification requests submitted by faculty to ensure accuracy.
  6. Generate Academic Reports: View and download student-wise, subject-wise, and class-wise academic performance reports.
  7. Subject Exam Analysis: Analyze performance distribution of students for a subject across exams using configurable mark ranges.
  8. Comparative Insights: Compare student performance across multiple subjects and exams to identify weak subjects and common low-performing students.
