HOD Responsibilities & Instructions

  1. Configure Branches & Subjects: Define branches, sections, and subjects for the academic years assigned to you.
  2. Approve Faculty Requests: Review subject allocation requests submitted by faculty members and approve or reject them based on departmental requirements.
  3. Manage Student Details: Add, update, and maintain student records including hall ticket numbers and names for each section.
  4. Create & Manage Exams: Add exams, modify schedules, and manage assessment structures for each section.
  5. Review Marks Update Requests: Verify and approve mark modification requests submitted by faculty to ensure data accuracy.
  6. Generate Academic Reports: Produce student-wise and class-wise performance reports for review and documentation.
  7. Analyze Performance Using Charts: Visualize subject-wise, student-wise, and comparative academic performance using interactive charts.
